# The Symphony of Cricket

Under the blazing golden sun,
Where dusty fields and dreams are one,
A game begins with silent grace,
Hope and fire on every face.

The bowler marks his careful stride,
With thunder hidden deep inside,
The crowd grows still, the moment tight,
As leather wings prepare for flight.

The batter stands with steady breath,
Balancing glory, fear, and death,
A single blink, a fleeting chance,
Can change the fate of this grand dance.

The ball swings wide through morning air,
A whisper moving swift and rare,
The willow speaks with perfect sound,
And sends pure magic through the ground.

The crowd erupts like ocean waves,
For heroes rise where courage braves,
A boundary raced across the green,
A shot so pure it feels unseen.

Cricket is more than runs and scores,
More than trophies, fame, or roars,
It lives in every narrow lane,
Where children play through joy and rain.

From broken bats and tennis balls,
To giant stadiums and halls,
The spirit stays forever bright,
A flame that burns through every night.

The spinner turns the world around,
Creating mystery from the ground,
The batter guesses far too late,
As stumps collapse beneath his fate.

The fielder dives with fearless art,
A moment born from endless heart,
Grass-stained hands and flying feet,
Where sacrifice and passion meet.

The scoreboard shifts with every over,
No battle truly ever over,
For cricket teaches those who fall,
To rise again and answer all.

There’s poetry inside each chase,
A test of nerve, a test of pace,
The final over, crowd in prayer,
Electric tension fills the air.

Some victories are carved in gold,
Some stories silently grow old,
Yet every match leaves something more,
A memory living at its core.

Legends walked these sacred grounds,
With timeless shots and roaring sounds,
Their names still echo strong and bright,
Like stars that guide the endless night.

And somewhere now a child still dreams,
Beside the rivers, fields, and streams,
Holding a bat with hopeful eyes,
Believing they too will someday rise.

For cricket is a beating song,
A place where every soul belongs,
A game of honor, grit, and flame,
Where millions proudly speak its name.

So let the wickets fall again,
Let sunlight mix with sudden rain,
For as long as hearts can dream and play,
Cricket will never fade away.
